 #Seven Then ⏳
A sleek and intuitive web-based application that helps you focus on the present by visualizing time in 7-minute intervals. Built with a modern tech stack for a smooth, responsive experience.

# ✨ Features
7-Minute Focus Timer: Break down your work or meditation into manageable, highly-focused 7-minute blocks.

Minimalist Interface: A clean, distraction-free UI that helps you concentrate on the task at hand.

Visual Time Progress: Watch a beautiful visual indicator as your 7 minutes elapse.

Audible Alerts: Get a gentle sound notification when your session is complete.

Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on your desktop, tablet, or mobile device.

Session Logging: (Optional Feature) Keep track of your completed focus sessions.

## 🎯 How to Use
Open the Seven Then app.

Click the "Start" button to begin a 7-minute focus session.

Focus on your task until the timer completes and the alert sounds.
