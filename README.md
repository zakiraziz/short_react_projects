✨ Inspiration & The "7-Minute" Philosophy
The concept of "Seven Then" is rooted in the idea that large tasks feel less daunting when broken into small, manageable chunks. Seven minutes is long enough to make meaningful progress on a task, but short enough to feel immediately achievable, helping to overcome procrastination and maintain a state of flow.

✨ Features
7-Minute Focus Timer: Break down your work or meditation into manageable, highly-focused 7-minute blocks.

Minimalist & Distraction-Free UI: A clean, calming interface designed to help you concentrate on the task at hand.

Visual Time Progress: Watch a beautiful, smooth visual indicator (like a circular progress bar) as your 7 minutes elapse.

Audible Alerts: Get a gentle, non-intrusive sound notification when your session is complete.

Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on your desktop, tablet, or mobile device.

Session Controls: Play, pause, and reset the timer with intuitive controls.

🎯 How to Use
Open the Seven Then app.

Click the "Start" button to begin a 7-minute focus session.

Focus intently on your single task until the timer completes.

When the alert sounds, take a short break (e.g., 2-3 minutes).

Reset the timer and start your next session!
